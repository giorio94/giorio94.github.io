About Me

I was born in Piedmont (Italy) in 1994. In 2016, I graduated cum laude from Politecnico di Torino with a Bachelor Degree in Computer Science. Then, I pursued my studies in the same university, achieving a Master Degree cum laude in 2018, with a thesis focused on cybersecurity aspects inherent vehicular networks.

Currently, I am a Ph.D. student at Politecnico di Torino, working on networking and security issues concerning vehicular technologies. My Ph.D. program is sponsored by Italdesign, a design and engineering company part of the Volkswagen Group.

Besides loving anything related to technology, I am interested in TV shows and I like relaxing with crossword puzzles and long walks in the countryside.
